We are a bioinformatics software engineering lab interested in applications of microbiome science in cancer prevention, cancer treatment, and environmental sustainability.

We additionally lead activities focused on bioinformatics education and training, and increasing diversity and inclusion in scientific computing.

Our software and educational content are free and open source, and we're committed to increasing accessibility and building community around reproducible bioinformatics and microbiome science.
